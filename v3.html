Diverse Classroom Libraries Project

We have a chance to expand classroom libraries so kids of color see themselves reflected and whiteness as the norm is interrupted for all kids. Parents will fund purchases, provide a curated list of books, acquire and distribute books.

The books have been selected according to these criteria:

  1. Fun reading. Not preachy. Will inspire the love of books.
  2. True to the experience of readers of color
  3. Broadens coverage of racial and ethnic groups
  4. Fresh. Recently published or not extremely well known.
  5. Good writing. Teach writing by example.
  6. At the right reading level

To participate, teachers should review the book list, then fill in the form below. The budget for each teacher is $50. Teachers can submit the form in print by putting it in the PPTG mailbox at Peralta, or digitally by sending an email to lucas@gonze.com. Teachers can request books not in the list, but please contact us first to verify that selections are in scope.
